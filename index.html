A brief background, and description:

Pixel Path: The Road to Code is a game built in order to help younger audiences (grades 5-8) get involved with programming. Our small team aims to fill the empty space of programming within the early school years, and help the younger generation gain an interest in programming.

This game follows our main character on the left, Pix, through a short story. All the while, it teaches you some fundamentals that can jumpstart your passion for programming!
